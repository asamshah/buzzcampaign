Our Statement and Methodology

Pickswise stands against all forms of online abuse and trolling. The purpose of this data is to highlight the level of abuse directed at sports professionals and to encourage social media companies to do more to combat the practice.

The data was collected directly from Twitter from the past 12 months (June 2020 – June 2021). Messages containing words, phrases and hashtags that were known abusive terms were logged against each player and the respective sport they play/ played in.
